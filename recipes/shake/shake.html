Banana & oat protein shake

Description

Shake packed with cals to help get the gain. Adjust the amount of oats for bigger gains. Protein powder flavour is up to you! Add any other fruits that you may like or even some peanut butter for more protein.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz skim milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 2 scoops of whey protein powder

Steps

  1. Chose your favorite protein powder flavor
  2. Combine all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth
  3. Serve into cup. Enjoy!
